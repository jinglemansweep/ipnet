What is MeshCore?

MeshCore is an open-source project that enables you to use inexpensive LoRa radios as a long-range mesh communication platform. Perfect for hiking, camping, or emergency communications.

Our local group maintains a network of nodes across Ipswich, Suffolk, UK, providing coverage and support for the community.
