Getting Started

Follow these steps to join our mesh network community

1

Join Discord

Connect with our community and introduce yourself in our Discord server.

2

Get Hardware

Purchase a compatible MeshCore/Meshtastic device (we can provide recommendations).

3

Configure Device

Set up your device with our network settings and test connectivity.

4

Join Network

Connect to the IPNet mesh and start communicating with other nodes!

Coverage Area

Our mesh network primarily covers Ipswich and surrounding areas in Suffolk, UK. We have nodes positioned strategically to provide the best coverage for the local community.

Current coverage includes:

  • Town Centre
  • IP2, IP3, IP4 and IP8
  • Selected outdoor locations
